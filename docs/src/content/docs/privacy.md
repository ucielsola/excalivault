# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 8, 2026

## Data Collection

Excalivault is a privacy-focused extension. We believe in local-first data storage.

### What We Collect

**We do NOT collect any data.** Period.

Your drawings and folders are stored **only** in your browser's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`). We do not:
- ❌ Send your drawings to any server
- ❌ Track usage or analytics
- ❌ Collect personal information
- ❌ Use cookies or third-party tracking

### What Data Is Stored Locally

All data stays on your device:

- **Drawings** - Elements, app state, and thumbnails
- **Folders** - Organization structure
- **Settings** - Your preferences

This data is never transmitted outside your browser.

## Permissions Required

Excalivault requests minimal permissions to function:

### `storage`
- **Purpose:** Save drawings and folders locally
- **Data Access:** Only your own Excalidraw data
- **Network Access:** None (data never leaves your device)

### `sidePanel`
- **Purpose:** Display the vault interface
- **Data Access:** UI rendering only
- **Network Access:** None

### `activeTab`
- **Purpose:** Access Excalidraw.com to save/load drawings
- **Data Access:** Only reads Excalidraw's localStorage
- **Network Access:** None

## Data Deletion

To delete all Excalivault data:

1. Open Chrome Settings → Extensions → Excalivault
2. Click "Remove"
3. All local data is automatically deleted

Alternatively, you can clear individual drawings or folders from the vault UI.

## Third-Party Services

Excalivault does not use any third-party services, APIs, or analytics.

## Excalidraw Integration

Excalivault integrates with [excalidraw.com](https://excalidraw.com):

- We only read/write to Excalidraw's localStorage
- No data is sent to Excalidraw's servers
- Excalidraw's own privacy policy applies when you use their platform
